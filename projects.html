  • Visualization
    • Oregon Health Authority HIV Cluster Dashboard

      • I developed this R Shiny dashboard while working with the Oregon Health Authority HIV/STD Division. The dashboard attached resembles what would have been used within meetings to explain and display vital information about HIV clusters in Oregon. To generate the graphs and charts, make sure to press "Run" on the legend in the left. The Date Range filters the data by year and the Cluster Number filter allows for you to select whichever cluster/combination of clusters you want. The data and information presented within the dashboard are randomly generated and do not represent real data that was used.

    • CA Notify Exposure Notification Dashboards
    • UCSD Small Business Outreach Project

      • I developed this Data Studio (now called Looker Studio) dashboard while working with the UC San Diego Business Outreach program where groups of volunteers called or visited local San Diego businesses to provide them information about operating practices and resources during the COVID-19 lockdowns. Outreach information was then recorded and visualized within this dashboard. The data and information presented within the dashboard are randomly generated and do not represent real data that was used.

      • A simple research project relating to the prevalence of COVID-19 and the median income within each county. This project involved my usage of ArcGIS to clean, organize, and visualize the data.
