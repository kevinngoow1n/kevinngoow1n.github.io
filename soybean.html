Meet Soybean

Midway through my undergraduate career, I adopted Soybean—a mischievous tuxedo cat who quickly became my sidekick in both work and life. Known for his notorious food adventures, Soybean has stolen hamburger buns straight from people’s hands, eaten through rolls of ciabatta bread, and developed a taste for human foods like pho, banh mi, kimbap, french fries, eggs, meatballs, chicken breast, and green beans.
Today, Soybean’s favorite treats are canned wet foods (meat-only, of course) and the occasional piece of bread (in moderation). Whether he’s stealing snacks or napping next to my computer, Soybean has been a constant source of humor and support through every milestone and challenge.

Take a look at his instagram page for more some more mischievousness!
