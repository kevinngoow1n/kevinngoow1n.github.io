CA Notify Exposure Notifications Visualizations

I developed some Data Studio (now called Looker Studio) dashboards while working under the CA Notify project for UC San Diego and the California Department of Public Health. The dashboards were used to display the current performance of the CA Notify system to UC San Diego and CDPH employees as well as the California governor at the time. When in use, the dashboard pulled data from various sources such as autopopulating Google Sheets (via webscraping and Apps Script), Google Analytics, SQL, AWS, and other databases developed by Apple and Google. The data and information presented within the dashboard are randomly generated and do not represent real data that was used.
